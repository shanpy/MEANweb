About Me

After graduated from The Ohio State University on Dec, 2013, I begin to work as a full-stack developer.

I'm very interested in web development skills. I've finished a J2EE (JSP/Servlet) web application development project for my current employer, from design to deployment.

In my free time, I like to learn more new and useful technology for web development. I began to design and work on this website from April, 2014 after read some books about Node.js and AngularJS. I was impressed about idea behind and decide to digging more into it. Thanks to Yeoman workflow, I was able to learn MEAN stack at the same time and use them for this website.

I also study Android development independently in school. In my current job, I develop an enterprise Android app for my employer. I'm good at creating test plans and writing technical documentations.

Thank you for visiting my website. Please feel free to check my blog or contact me on LinkedIn.
